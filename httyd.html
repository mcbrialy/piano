How to Train Your Dragon Medley

How to Train Your Dragon is one of my favorite movies. I’ve watched it at least 30 times and can recite every line of dialogue along with the movie (which yes, is probably very annoying for those watching it with me). The soundtrack of this movie is unrivaled. I found this medley online, and it combines the most iconic songs from the movie into one. I’m going to compare the medley to “Test Drive,” since that’s the song it begins with. The other songs included in this medley are “Forbidden Friendship,” “Romantic Flight,” and “Where’s Hiccup?”

DIFFICULTY RATING:

5/10

Pros:

  • Medium length – doesn’t take long to learn
  • Tempo is relatively easy to keep up with

Cons:

  • Multiple key changes
  • Tricky fingerings

I love this medley because it is an accurate portrayal of how the soundtrack sounded. It combines the best parts of the best songs. The ending is sweet, somber, and finishes the piece perfectly. It’s somewhat simple to learn yet still brings the nostalgia and tugs at your heartstrings.
